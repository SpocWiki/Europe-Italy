> The Tyrrhenian Sea (tih-REE-nee-ən ,-RAY-; Italian: Mar Tirreno [mar tirˈrɛːno] or [-ˈreː-]) 
> is part of the Mediterranean Sea off the western coast of Italy. 
> 
> It is named for the Tyrrhenian people identified with the Etruscans of Italy.
>
